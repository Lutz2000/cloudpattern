Frequently Asked Questions

What are your office hours?

Our standard office hours are Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our emergency contact number.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we offer a free 30-minute initial consultation to discuss your needs and how we can help. Book your consultation through our website or by calling our office.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, mobile money (MTN, Airtel), credit/debit cards, and cash payments at our office.

Can I schedule an in-person meeting?

Absolutely! You can schedule an in-person meeting at our Kampala office by calling us or using the booking system on our website.
